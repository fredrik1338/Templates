# Operational Information

This folder contains information that stakeholders outside the team may need during incidents or for planning.

## Suggested Documents

### Dependencies & Architecture
- `dependencies.md` - Services we depend on (upstream) and services that depend on us (downstream)
- `kill-chain.md` - Critical path analysis showing impact of failures
- `architecture-overview.md` - High-level system diagram and component descriptions

### Contacts & Escalation
- `contacts.md` - Team members, roles, and contact information
- `escalation-path.md` - Who to contact for different severity levels
- `communication-channels.md` - Slack channels, email lists, bridge lines

### Schedules & Availability
- `on-call-schedule.md` - Current on-call rotation and how to reach on-call
- `support-hours.md` - When the team is available and response time expectations
- `holiday-coverage.md` - Coverage during holidays and special periods

### Service Level Objectives
- `sla-summary.md` - Recovery objectives (RPO/RTO) for each service
- `critical-services.md` - Prioritized list of services by business impact
